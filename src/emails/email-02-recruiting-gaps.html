Why recruiting campaigns lose candidates
Many recruiting campaigns struggle to convert interest into applications.
In most cases, the issue isn’t traffic—it’s the lack of a structured engagement system behind the campaign.
Common gaps include:
• Inconsistent follow-up
• Lack of segmentation
• Limited visibility into engagement
Without a connected workflow, candidate interest often drops off before meaningful engagement occurs.
