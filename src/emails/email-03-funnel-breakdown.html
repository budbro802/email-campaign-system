Where most hiring funnels break down
Candidate drop-off typically happens between initial interest and application completion.
This gap is often caused by a lack of structured follow-up and automation within the recruiting process.
When systems are not connected:
1. Candidate intent is not captured effectively
2. Follow-up communication is delayed or inconsistent
3. Engagement data is not leveraged for future outreach
As a result, qualified candidates disengage before entering the pipeline.
